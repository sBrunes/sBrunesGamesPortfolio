About Me!
Hello! My name's Sam, and I’m a technical game designer! I’ve been making games for a bit over 5 year, and have loved (almost) every second of it! I'm proficient in both Unreal Engine 5 and Unity, and specialize in iterative design and gameplay prototyping.
I graduated with my Master's in Human Computer Interaction in 2024, which has really helped me develop a focus on expected player-experience during my design process.
My favorite games include FPS and Strategy games, like Halo and Slay the Spire! Lately, I've also fallen in love with Ocarina of Time and Majora's Mask, after playing through them with my wife!
